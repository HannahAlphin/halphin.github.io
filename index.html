My Favorite Ed Tech Tools

  1. Articulate Storyline 360 I am very familar with Storyline 360 because I use it at my job. It is a great software for creating interactive eLearning content.
  2. Camtasia I used Camtasia a lot during my undergrad to edit videos for projects. I like how easy it is to learn and navigate.
  3. Powtoon I have used Powtoon in the past to create animated explainer videos. I enjoy how it allows you to add characters and graphics to make your videos more visually interesting.
